## Full description

Rasoi helps you discover Indian home-style recipes that fit your life.

Pick breakfast, lunch, dinner, or an evening snack. Choose a cuisine—North Indian, South Indian, or Indo-Chinese—or leave it open. Tell us how long you want to cook, and get a recipe with ingredients, steps, and approximate nutrition.

**Personalize it**

Set your diet (vegetarian, non-vegetarian, or custom), goals, activity level, and allergies in Your profile. Everything stays on your device until you ask for a new recipe.

**Stay varied**

Meal history remembers your last suggestions so you are less likely to see the same dish again.

**Important**

Recipe suggestions are generated with AI for general wellness and cooking inspiration. They are not medical advice. Always check ingredients for allergens and use safe cooking practices.
